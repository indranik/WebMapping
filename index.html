Earthquake Mapping

Introduction

According to the National Earthquake Information Center, about 12,000-14,000 earthquakes occur each year!. On average, Magnitude 2 and smaller earthquakes occur several hundred times a day world wide. Major earthquakes, greater than magnitude 7, happen more than once per month. "Great earthquakes", magnitude 8 and higher, occur about once a year.

This interactive map shows the location and details of the earthquakes that occurred in the last one week.
